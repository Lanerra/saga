Jules discovers the Veil Shatterer is a keystone in the city's magical system that demands a sacrifice from the lower districts each time it is used, revealing his unwitting role in perpetuating the cycle of oppression. Elian confirms the artifact's true nature and the cost of its power, forcing Jules to confront his complicity and choose a new path. Jules resolves to break the cycle without taking another life, marking his transformation from smuggler to revolutionary.
