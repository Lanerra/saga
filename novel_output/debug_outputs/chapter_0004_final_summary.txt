In Chapter 4, Jules discovers Kael has been betraying the resistance by leaking information to an external source, revealing the mole is within their ranks. Tula, a key resistance leader, confronts Jules with accusations of his own complicity, forcing him to question his trust in those he once believed were allies. The revelation shatters Jules's faith in the mission and the people he fought alongside, leaving him grappling with betrayal and the cost of loyalty.
