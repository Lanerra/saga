Virell discovers the Aether Resonator is not just a machine but a gateway that awakens trapped souls and ancient forces, as the echoes of the past grow stronger and more personal. He encounters mysterious symbols and a spectral figure warning him that he has disturbed something beyond his control, deepening his realization that the Resonator is a key to an unknown power. As he advances into the Veilspire Pass, the line between his mind and the machine blurs, forcing him to confront the terrifying truth of what he has unleashed.
