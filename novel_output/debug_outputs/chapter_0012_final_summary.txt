Kaelen activates the Loop's core, breaking its cycle and causing the colony to collapse, revealing the truth behind the Chrono Syndicate's control. The group faces the uncertainty of their past and future as they step into the Veil Of Drift, each member grappling with their identity and the consequences of freeing themselves from the illusion. Kaelen chooses truth over the prison of repetition, setting them on a path to redefine their reality.
