Jules realizes the Veil Shatterer is not just a tool but a keystone that feeds the curse by draining life from the lower districts, forcing him to confront his complicity. He seeks the Mirror of Equilibrium to break the curse, discovering his own name in a ledger of stolen lives and deciding to face the truth instead of running. The Mirror, hidden beneath the Exchange, represents a chance to dismantle the system of exploitation that has shaped his life.
