Professor Virell Durn activates the Aether Resonator, a machine designed to transcend time, but it awakens ancient echoes and whispers from the past, unsettling him and revealing his fear of losing himself to the very knowledge he sought. The workshop transforms into something alive, and Virell begins to question his identity and the cost of his ambition. He leaves the workshop, determined to find the Veilspire Pass to understand what he has unleashed.
