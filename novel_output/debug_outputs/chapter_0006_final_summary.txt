Sága and Lyra discover a hidden chamber in Eternis containing a crystalline core that reveals the star was never a weapon but a sentient guardian, challenging the Council's long-held beliefs. Their discovery forces Sága to confront her role in the Council's deception and shift from survival to revolutionary action, determined to free the star and expose the truth. The Reclaimers now understand the true purpose of Eternis and the need to act against the Council's lies.
